Shaikh Zayed Garnd Masjid

The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is located in Abu Dhabi, UAE. It is one of the largest mosques in the world, accommodating over 41,000 worshippers. The mosque was completed in 2007 after 11 years of construction. It features 82 domes and over 1,000 columns. The mosque’s chandeliers are gilded with 24-carat gold. It houses the world’s largest hand-knotted carpet.
