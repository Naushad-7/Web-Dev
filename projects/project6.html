Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic mixed rice dish that is immensely popular in South Asia, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. It typically consists of rice, meat (such as chicken, goat, lamb, beef, prawn, or fish), and a variety of spices. There are also vegetarian versions that use vegetables or paneer instead of meat
